Understanding Risk Profiles

Low Risk

Annual Rainfall: < 1500mm

Humidity: Low (< 72%)

Visibility: Clear (> 35km)

Medium Risk

Annual Rainfall: ~ 2000mm

Humidity: Moderate (~ 75%)

Visibility: Hazy (~ 25km)

High Risk

Annual Rainfall: > 3000mm

Humidity: High (> 78%)

Visibility: Poor (< 15km)
